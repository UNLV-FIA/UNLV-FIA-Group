Meet Our Team


The UNLV-FIA group typically employs 15 to 25+ people with a variety of backgrounds, skillsets, and levels of expertise. In addition to full-time staff, our team includes part-time data specialists retired from the Forest Service and student interns. Major areas of emphasis include data/database architecture, system/database administration, computer systems analysis, software engineering, project management, technical writing, and accessibility testing and remediation.

Brenda J. Buck
Program Director

Brenda J. Buck - Program Director
Work Location: Remote
Email: buckb@unlv.nevada.edu

Dr. Brenda J. Buck joined the UNLV-FIA program in 2017. As the Program Director and Principal Investigator, she oversees daily operations.

Dr. Buck received her Ph.D. in 1996 from New Mexico State University in Agronomy/Soil Science. She has been a professor at UNLV since 1998. Dr. Buck’s research has spanned a wide range of interdisciplinary topics. She is perhaps best known for medical geology research on health hazards associated with dust exposure. Earlier work included other applications of arid soil science/geology. Dr. Buck has served as a researcher, collaborator, and teacher for field expeditions to 18 countries on 6 continents. She is also an artist, outdoor enthusiast, cyclist, and loves live music.
