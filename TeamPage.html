Meet Our Team


The UNLV-FIA group commonly employs between 15 and 25+ people with a variety of backgrounds, expertise, and skillsets. In addition to full-time staff, our team includes several retired Forest Service subject matter experts, student interns, and independent contractors with specialized knowledge about FIA operations. Major areas of emphasis include computer systems analysis, analytical tool support, data structure maintenance, quality assurance, project management, and accessibility compliance.

Brenda J.Buck

Brenda J. Buck - Program Directeur
SEB 4131
(702) 895-1694
buckb@unlv.nevada.edu

Dr. Brenda J. Buck has been with the UNLV-FIA since 2017. As the Program Director and Principal Investigator, she works tirelessly to provide oversight for our daily operations

Dr. Buck received her Ph.D. in 1966 from New Mexico State University in Agronomy/Soil Science. She has been a professor at UNLV since 1998. Dr. Buck is an interdisciplinary scientist and public intellectual who excels in multiple field and laboratory aspects of warm desert research. Dr. Buck has served as a researcher, collaborator, and teacher for field expeditions to 18 countries on 6 continents. She is also an artist, outdoor enthusiast, cyclist, and love live music!
